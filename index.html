About Joanne

Joanne designed for her own eveningwear company in New York City for 15 years. Her designs appeared on the covers and pages of Harper’s Bazaar and Women’s Wear Daily to name a few.

After moving to Celebration, Florida to raise her family, she transferred her years of design experience to interiors, launching Joanne King Design in 2003. Her unique combination of talents has resulted in her clients expressing deep gratitude for the soothing, beautiful and comfortable environments she has created for them.

Joanne believes that a more peaceful life can be achieved through the proper selection, combination, and placement of paint colors, lighting, art, furniture, window treatments… and the practice of yoga.
